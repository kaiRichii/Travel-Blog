TRAVEL BLOG

Do you ever have that sensation before a grand vacation? You get excited and start planning what you'll wear, what you'll bring, where you'll eat, where to take a TikTok video, snap an Instagram photo, and all the other fun things. Whether you've been there before or not, you're either excited to explore the place or unwind. I think we consider these things one of the best feelings to experience. Besides, according to a new study, booking travel generates happiness and can alleviate stress and uncertainty during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Travel is essential, and everyone should do it every once in a while because it can help reduce stress, be educational, and can help make memories last a lifetime. It relieves stress because, whether you spend your time hiking, swimming, or just sitting on the seashore, doing the things you enjoy the most will reduce stress. Furthermore, you experience your freedom more because you can disregard any schedule and do the things you've always wanted to do.

It is educational because it introduces you to different cuisines and cultures, as well as historical events of a place. For instance, one of our dream destinations to visit here in the Philippines is the Intramuros in Metro Manila. If we travel there one day, we will understand its significance and the story behind its historic structures and gates thanks to the tour guides there. Besides, According to Emma Galasso, travel is an epitome of experiential learning, making it one of the many reasons travel is the best education money can buy.

Lastly, it can help make memories last a lifetime because it creates stronger relationships or bonds. Traveling with family or friends allows you to turn special memories into fascinating stories that will last a lifetime. We are more relaxed and open to showing our true selves when traveling, which adds to the fun and will create a bond like no other. There will be times when you encounter unexpected obstacles on your journey. However, once you figure out how to overcome them, those memories are the life lessons you have had the opportunity to learn.
